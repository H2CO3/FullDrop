Development

  • FullDrop is an iOS application which was designed to enable you to fully access your Dropbox account from your iPhone, including uploading any types of file (which is a serious limitation of the official Dropbox app).

    Thanks for the Dropbox Team for releasing the iOS Objective-C API client and for approving my app.
